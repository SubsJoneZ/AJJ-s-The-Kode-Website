Top 3 Reasons to Collaborate

1) ENHANCE YOUR PRODUCTIVITY: Did you know that the biggest and most profitable tech giants known to man started in a common place... A school, a place of business, a birthday party, etc? Networking doesn't have to be this big event. Sometimes all we have to do is commit to working together nomatter how small the project. Synergy is guaranteed to produce something. Statistically, synergy is undefeated. Connecting with a team of developers will enhance your synergy.

2) If starting your own company is the reason you take interest in coding, then you definitely will benefit from someone who has the general knowledge of digital marketing. To me and my business, an associate who's a web developer is like my personal digital marketing consuntant. They can teach you about SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and help you implement it. If you need someone to help you build the app or a website for your business, and teach you how to do it yourself, you can't lose!

3) Imagine having millions of mentors. On the internet you do. Everyday I search "how to" before receiving information provided by at least 85% of the entire tech community. Including the really rich ones. Next time you search Google, remember that it is a giant complex in California that pays it's 140,000 employees a top tier salary to send us valuable information on the web for free.
